  <div class="text">
    <h3>Shaky 3-RPR</h3>
    <p>
      The structure shall be rigid (imagine pneumatic actuators in the legs), however it is shaky (moving in a range) because the direct kinematics solutions coicide. 
      It was proposed by Manfred Husty. 
    </p>
  </div>
</div>
